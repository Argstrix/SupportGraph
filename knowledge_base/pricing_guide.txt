# ABC Technologies - Pricing Guide & Product Information

ABC Technologies offers cloud-based business management software tailored for Indian businesses,
from small startups to large enterprises. All pricing is in Indian Rupees (INR) with GST applicable
as per Indian tax regulations.

## 1. Starter Plan
- **Price**: ₹2,499 per month (or ₹24,990 billed annually — save 17%). GST extra as applicable.
- **Target**: Freelancers, small businesses, and startups with lean teams.
- **User Limit**: Up to 5 users.
- **Features**:
  - Basic business management tools (task boards, scheduling, attendance tracking).
  - 5 GB secure cloud storage hosted on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) region.
  - Standard email support — response within 24 business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM IST).
  - Integration with popular Indian payment gateways: Razorpay, Paytm Business.
  - GST-compliant invoice generation.

## 2. Growth Plan
- **Price**: ₹6,999 per month (or ₹69,990 billed annually — save 17%). GST extra as applicable.
- **Target**: Growing SMEs (Small and Medium Enterprises) and mid-sized teams across India.
- **User Limit**: Up to 25 users.
- **Features**:
  - Advanced business management, billing automations, and CRM integration.
  - 50 GB secure cloud storage on AWS Mumbai region.
  - Priority email and live chat support — response within 4 business hours (IST).
  - Custom reports, exportable analytics, and workflow automations.
  - Integration with Tally, Zoho Books, and Indian banking APIs.
  - Automated TDS and GST filing report generation.

## 3. Enterprise Plan
- **Price**: ₹17,999 per month (or ₹1,79,990 billed annually — save 17%). GST extra as applicable.
- **Target**: Large Indian corporations, listed companies, and businesses with complex compliance needs.
- **User Limit**: Unlimited users.
- **Features**:
  - Full-suite business management, ERP integration (SAP, Oracle), and REST API access.
  - 500 GB secure cloud storage with data residency guarantee (India region only).
  - 24/7 dedicated phone and email support — response within 1 hour (including IST weekends).
  - Dedicated account manager (India-based) and onboarding specialist.
  - Custom Service Level Agreement (SLA) with Indian law jurisdiction.
  - Enterprise security compliance: SSO/SAML, audit logs, RBI-compliant data handling.
  - Advanced GST, TDS, PF, and ESI payroll compliance modules.

## 4. Free Trial & Sandbox
- A 14-day free trial is available for both the Starter and Growth plans. No credit/debit card required.
- Sandbox testing environments are available on request for Enterprise accounts.
- Trial accounts come pre-loaded with sample Indian business data for realistic testing.

## 5. Payment Methods Accepted
- UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm)
- Net banking (all major Indian banks)
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- NEFT / RTGS for Enterprise annual plans
- EMI options available on select credit cards (6/12 month tenure)
