# ABC Technologies - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Q: How do I reset my password?
**A**: To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your
registered email address or mobile number, and we will send you a password reset link via email
and an OTP to your registered mobile number. Follow the instructions to set a new password.
Your new password must be at least 12 characters long and include uppercase, lowercase, a digit,
and a special character (e.g., !, @, #, $).

## Q: What should I do if I forget my username or login email?
**A**: Please contact Account Support. To verify your identity, you will need to provide your
full name, registered mobile number, company name, and GSTIN (if applicable). Our team will
help you recover your login credentials within 1–2 business hours (IST).

## Q: Can I get a refund for my subscription?
**A**: We offer a full refund if requested within 30 days of the purchase date. Please submit a
refund request through the support portal or by contacting Billing Support. All refund requests
are reviewed and approved by a human supervisor before processing. Approved refunds are credited
back to your original payment method (UPI, net banking, or card) within 5–7 business days.
Annual plan refunds after 30 days are considered on a case-by-case basis.

## Q: How do I change my subscription plan?
**A**: Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan and select the plan you wish to upgrade or downgrade
to. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rated billing for the remaining cycle. Downgrades
take effect from the next billing cycle. All prices are in INR and inclusive of applicable GST.

## Q: How can I update my profile details?
**A**: Log in to your dashboard and click on your profile icon (top-right corner) > Settings >
Profile. You can update your name, contact details, profile picture, mobile number, and
notification preferences. To update your company's GSTIN or billing address, go to Settings >
Company Profile > Tax Details.

## Q: My account is deactivated. How do I reactivate it?
**A**: If your account was deactivated due to a missed payment, simply update your payment method
in Settings > Billing > Payment Methods and complete the pending payment — your account will
be reactivated automatically within 15 minutes. If your account was deactivated for security
reasons or by request, please contact Account Support. Identity verification via Aadhaar-linked
mobile OTP may be required.

## Q: Do you provide GST-compliant invoices?
**A**: Yes. All invoices issued by ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd. are GST-compliant and include our
GSTIN (27AABCA1234B1ZX). You can download tax invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.
For B2B customers, ensure your company GSTIN is updated in your account profile for proper ITC
(Input Tax Credit) claims.

## Q: Is my data stored in India?
**A**: Yes. All customer data is stored exclusively on servers located in the AWS Mumbai region
(ap-south-1) within India. This ensures compliance with India's data localisation requirements.
Your data is never transferred outside India without your explicit written consent.

## Q: What are your support working hours?
**A**: Our support team operates Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM IST.
Enterprise plan customers enjoy 24/7 dedicated support, including Sundays and Indian public holidays.
Response times: Starter — 24 hours | Growth — 4 hours | Enterprise — 1 hour.

## Q: Can I pay using UPI or EMI?
**A**: Yes. We accept UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM), net banking, credit/debit cards
(Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and NEFT/RTGS for annual Enterprise plans. EMI options (6 or 12 months,
no-cost EMI on select credit cards) are available during checkout for plans above ₹6,999/month.
