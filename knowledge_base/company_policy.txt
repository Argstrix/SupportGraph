# ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is registered under the Companies Act, 2013 (CIN: U72900MH2018PTC123456),
headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. All policies are governed by Indian law and are subject to
the jurisdiction of courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

## 1. Refund Policy
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of their initial purchase if they are
  unsatisfied with the product.
- Refund requests after 30 days are handled on a case-by-case basis and require human supervisor review.
- All refund requests — regardless of the purchase date — must be reviewed and approved by a human
  supervisor to prevent fraud and ensure proper processing per RBI guidelines.
- Approved refunds will be credited back to the customer's original payment method within 5–7 business
  days (excluding Indian bank holidays and public holidays).
- For UPI or net banking refunds, processing may take up to 3 additional business days depending on
  the customer's bank.
- Refunds are processed in INR and are subject to applicable GST reversal as per Indian tax laws.

## 2. Subscription Cancellation
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time through their account dashboard under
  Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
- If a customer requests cancellation via customer support, the support executive must verify the
  customer's identity using registered mobile number OTP or email confirmation.
- Any request for immediate subscription cancellation with billing adjustments must be escalated
  and approved by a human supervisor.
- Upon cancellation, customers will retain access to their plan until the end of the current
  billing cycle. No pro-rated refund is issued for the remaining days of the cycle.
- Annual plan cancellations are eligible for a partial refund only if requested within 30 days
  of renewal, subject to supervisor approval.

## 3. Account Closure Policy
- Account closure results in the permanent and irreversible deletion of all customer data,
  including configuration history, documents, invoices, and stored files.
- As per India's Personal Data Protection guidelines, customers have the right to request
  data erasure, but this action cannot be undone.
- Account closure requests cannot be processed automatically and must be routed to a human
  supervisor for manual validation and final approval.
- A mandatory 7-day cooling-off period applies after supervisor approval, during which the
  customer may cancel the closure request.

## 4. Compensation and Credits
- In the event of system downtime, service outages, or SLA breach, customers may request
  compensation or service credits.
- Support agents are not authorised to issue compensation or credits on their own authority.
- All compensation and service credit requests must be escalated to a human supervisor for approval.
- Credits issued will appear as a balance adjustment in the customer's billing dashboard within
  2 business days and will be auto-applied to the next invoice.

## 5. Escalation to Management
- If a customer is highly dissatisfied, demands to speak to a senior manager, or threatens
  legal action or consumer court proceedings (under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019), the
  support ticket must be escalated immediately.
- Escalation to management requires a human supervisor's routing and approval.
- Escalated cases will be acknowledged within 4 business hours (IST) and resolved within
  5 business days.

## 6. Data Privacy (IT Act & PDPB Compliance)
- All customer data is stored on servers located within India (AWS Mumbai region) in compliance
  with data localisation requirements.
- ABC Technologies does not share customer data with third parties without explicit written consent,
  except as required by Indian law enforcement agencies under valid court orders.
- Customers may request a copy of their stored data at any time by submitting a Data Access Request
  via the support portal.
